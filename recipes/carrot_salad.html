Carrot Salad

Quick, zesty salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • Medium white onion
  • Red pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Juice from 1/2 a lemon

Instructions

  1. Cut the onions in to thin half moons and the pepper in to thin strips
  2. Grate the carrot
  3. Mix everything together in a bowl or lock-tight and leave to marinate on juice
