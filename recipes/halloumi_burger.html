Halloumi burger

Halloumi burger with red onion pickle

Ingredients

For the pickle

  • 3 medium red onions
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Half bunch of dill
  • 1 tbsp black onion seeds
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the buger

  • Block of halloumi
  • Red onion pickle
  • 2 brioche buns
  • Rocket
  • Mayonaise
  • 2 tsp Cajun spice
  • Olive oil

Instructions

For the pickle

  1. Chop the onions in to thin half moons
  2. In a bowl and using your hand, combine the onions, salt and sugar until dissolved
  3. Add chopped dill, vinegar and seeds. Stir.
  4. Leave for at least 20 mins, preferably overnight.

For the burger

  1. Put the skillet on a medium heat and turn on the grill
  2. Mix cajun spice and oil in a wide bowl
  3. Cut the halloumi in to 6 pieces and marinate in the spiced oil
  4. Fry the halloumi for 1-2 mins on each side
  5. While halloumi is cooking, toast the buns under the grill.
  6. Layer mayo, rocket, halloumi and pickle between the buns
