Spinach Pasta

Home made pasta in a spinach and cream sauce

I just got a new pasta machine so decided to try it out. The sauce is really just a vehicle to showcase the pasta, which I've never made before

Ingredients

For the pasta

  • 100g 00 flour
  • 1 large egg
  • salt

For the sauce

  • half a small white onion, minced
  • ~10 black olives, halved
  • handful of spinach
  • 50ml whole milk
  • 70ml cream
  • 2tsp butter
  • 2 tsp flour
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Half a chicken stock pot
  • Slice of lemon

Instructions

For the pasta

  1. Create a mound of the flour on a clean surface, add a pinch of salt
  2. Create a crator in the flour large enough to hold the egg
  3. Crack the egg in the crator
  4. Mix the flour in to the middle until the egg is completely incorporated
  5. Knead until solf and silky (at least 15mins)
  6. Shape in to a ball and leave, covered, for at least 30 mins to relax
  7. After relaxing, put through pasta machine and leave to dry a little.
  8. Cook in boiling, heavily salted water for 2mins

For the sauce

  1. Place skillet over a medium heat and melt butter
  2. Put the spinach in a colinder and pour over boiling water to wilt. Leave to cool until safe to touch.
  3. Add onion and cook until transluscent
  4. Add the garlic and olives, cook for ~ 1 min
  5. Add flour and cook out, ~ 1 min
  6. Slowly add the milk, stirring constantly until a smooth paste is formed
  7. Add in the cream and stock pot
  8. Squeeze out excess water from spinach and finely chop. Add to sauce.
  9. Add in the pasta along with some of the lovely pasta water, stir
  10. Add a squeeze of lemon and season to taste
